1. Introduction

ColsenX (CXP) is a multi-purpose Web3 utility token launched on Binance Smart Chain (BSC). Designed for use in lifestyle, finance, and creator-focused applications, CXP enables decentralized access to payments, trading, and community growth.

2. Vision and Mission

Vision: To create a trusted Web3 token that bridges lifestyle, finance, and creator tools for real-world daily use.

Mission:

  • Launch a secure and accessible token on BSC
  • Enable CXP for swaps, payments, and tipping
  • Grow a self-sustaining ecosystem for traders and creators
